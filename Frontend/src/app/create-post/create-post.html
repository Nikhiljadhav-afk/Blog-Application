Markdown Formatting Guide:

  • Headings: # for main title, ## for subtitles, ### for sections
  • Bold: **text**, __text__, or ***text***
  • Italic: *text* or _text_
  • Lists: - or * for bullet points, 1. for numbered lists
  • Links: [text](url)
  • Code: `code` for inline, ```code``` for blocks
  • Horizontal lines: --- or *** or ___
  • Line breaks: Add two spaces at end of line or use blank lines between paragraphs
